2015 Daily deaths by police

The data point examined here is to determine whether there are any trends regarding the amount of deaths caused by police, as in if there are any periods of time where it is more prone to occuring.

Deaths by Police in 2015 by Type

An interesting datapoint here is by what these deaths are caused by. A far majority of the deaths are by shootings, however, the next most frequent cause of death by police is tasered, which is presented as a non-lethal means of keeping order.

Deaths by Police in 2015 by Gender

This page shows a ring chart with the proportion of suspects killed by Gender, where a high majority of persons killed by police are Male.
